  <div class="section">
    <h2>Our Mission</h2>
    <p>We are a nonprofit organization dedicated to bringing essential health services to underserved communities across South Africa. Our goal is to close the healthcare access gap for rural populations by delivering education, care, and empowerment directly to those in need.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="section">
    <h2>What We Do</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Deploy mobile medical clinics to rural villages</li>
      <li>Partner with local community leaders and clinics</li>
      <li>Educate the public on sanitation, nutrition, and preventative care</li>
      <li>Distribute essential supplies and medication</li>
      <li>Advocate for healthcare rights in South Africa's rural zones</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="section">
    <h2>Inspired by Global Movements</h2>
    <p>We draw inspiration from Doctors Without Borders, customizing their humanitarian model to fit the rural South African context. Our interventions are driven by compassion, dignity, and the belief that healthcare is a universal human right.</p>
    <img src="https://www.doctorswithoutborders.org/themes/custom/dwb_theme/images/photos/field-team.jpg" alt="Field Doctors" style="width:100%;height:auto;">
  </div>

  <div class="section">
    <h2>Why It Matters</h2>
    <p>Millions of South Africans in rural areas still lack access to basic healthcare. We step in where the traditional system falls short, creating a people-centered network that puts health and humanity first. By volunteering, donating, or spreading awareness, you help us reach farther.</p>
