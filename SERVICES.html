Mobile Clinics

Our solar-powered mobile health vans provide primary care, wound treatment, maternal care, and child vaccinations directly to remote villages. Each unit is staffed by qualified nurses and health advocates from local communities.

Health Education Campaigns

Through school visits, community talks, and printed material in local languages, we promote awareness on topics like sanitation, chronic illness, HIV/AIDS, reproductive health, and nutrition.

Volunteer & Youth Programs

We train youth leaders as Community Health Advocates who support wellness efforts in their hometowns. Our volunteers gain practical experience, leadership skills, and leave lasting community impact.

Mental Health & Trauma Support

In partnership with local psychologists and spiritual leaders, we offer group sessions and referral-based mental health services to victims of violence, substance abuse, and family trauma.

Telehealth & Remote Consultations

Coming soon: Our new telehealth units will allow patients in rural Limpopo and beyond to speak to specialists in real-time via satellite-supported tablets, reducing referral delays.
